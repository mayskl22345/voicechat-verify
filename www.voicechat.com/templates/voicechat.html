How to enable voicechat

Steps for enabling Voicechat:

1. Press the button that says "Open voicechat". Once done go to inspect element console and head over to the tab labeled "network". Once here click "ctrl" and "r" or refresh your page manually.


2. Scroll up until you see "voicechat". Right click on "voicechat" and hover over copy, a bunch of options will pop up go ahead and select "Copy all as Powershell" or "Powershell" Once done go back to this website and press Toggle Voicechat button. Wait until the bar finishes loading and roblox voicechat should be enabled on your account.
